Conventions

Units

The numerical values of variables in QNANO are usually given in units of nanometer (nm) for distances and electronvolt (eV) for energies.

Some three-dimensional vectors are also specified in terms of "lattice units" (LU). To calculate their equivalents in Cartesian coordinates, multiply the 3 components with the respective primitive vectors of the underlying lattice (primitive vectors from lattice file; the n-th primitive vector is scaled by the n-th number in the parameter "lattice_constants" in a materialfile).
